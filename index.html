About

I am a third year PhD student at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) in the Department of Computer Science. My advisors are Alyson Fletcher and Sundeep Rangan.

My current research focus is on understanding the dynamics of training deep generative models in high-dimensional regimes. I am especially interested in the implicit bias and regularization of deep learning, as well as uncovering necessary conditions for convergence and generalization.

Previously I was a Stamps Scholar at the University of Pittsburgh and earned a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering in 2020.
